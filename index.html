Fatigue-aware overload hints
Progressive overload suggestions that account for how many same-muscle exercises you've already done. Bench press after 3 chest movements isn't the same as going first.
Smart
🏆
Fatigue-adjusted PRs
1RM estimates that account for how many sets preceded your top set. A PR hit on set 4 after warm-ups is adjusted upward — because you could do more fresh.
Smart
📊
Per-set logging
Log individual sets with reps and weight. Mark failed sets with a simple 'f' suffix. Volume is calculated from actual sets logged — not averaged or estimated.

Session-based programs
Build programs from your saved workouts. A "week" completes when you've done all your sessions — Push, Pull, Legs — regardless of how many calendar days it took.
